> WIP

- https://sw.kovidgoyal.net/kitty/conf/#keyboard-shortcuts

| keys  | action
| ---   | ---
| Ctrl+Shift+T      | New tab
| Ctrl+Shift+arrows | Move across tabs
| Ctrl+Shift+ENTER  | New pane
| Ctrl+Shift+l      | Change pane orientation
| Ctrl+Shift+f      | Change pane order
