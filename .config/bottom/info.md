| bind          | action
| :--           | :--
| e             | expand widget
| Ctrl+DirKeys  | Focus to other widget
| H J K L       | Focus to other widget
| W A S D       | Focus to other widget
| +, -, =       | zoom in/out/reset -- time range
| P             | full Process command
| /             | search Processes
| Tab           | group Processes
| s \| F6       | sort Processes
| t \| F5       | tree Processes
| d + d \| F9   | kill Process
